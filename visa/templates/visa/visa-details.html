Frequently Asked Questions

What is a visa, and why do I need one?

A visa is an official document that allows you to enter, stay in, and leave a foreign country. It is typically stamped or glued into your passport. Whether you need a visa depends on your nationality and the country you wish to visit. A visa ensures that you meet the entry requirements for your destination.

How can I apply for a visa through ShadhinTrip?

You can apply for a visa through ShadhinTrip by visiting our website and selecting the visa type you need. Our platform will guide you through the application process, including document submission and payment. Our team is also available to assist you with any questions you might have during the process.

Can I get a refund if my visa application is denied?

Unfortunately, visa fees and the ShadhinTrip processing fee are non-refundable, as they cover the administrative costs associated with processing your application.

How long does it take to process a visa?

The processing time for a visa depends on the type of visa and the destination country. Some visas are processed within a few days, while others may take several weeks. We recommend applying well in advance of your planned travel date to avoid any delays.

Do I need a visa if I am just transiting through a country?

Some countries require a transit visa if you are passing through their territory on the way to another destination. It is essential to verify the transit visa requirements for all the countries on your travel route.
